Data sharing

Zenithoche does not sell user data. Information may be shared with service providers only as needed to operate the service, process payments, host websites, provide support, or comply with law.

Zenithoche keeps information for as long as needed to operate products, provide support, maintain records, prevent abuse, or meet legal and payment obligations. Some Discord server data may remain until removed by server configuration, support request, or product cleanup.
